Prince Archie plays saucepan drums in sweet home video with mum Meghan Markle

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 12, 2024

The Duke and Duchess of Sussex's son Prince Archie has showcased his musical talents by playing the piano, and in another sweet home video, the tot plays a saucepan as a drum.

The candid family moment was shown in episode five of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle's Netflix docuseries, titled Harry & Meghan.

The little one uses wooden sticks to bang a yellow saucepan, and he can be heard asking his mother: "Was that a good song?" to which his mom replies, "That was such a good song, it was awesome." "Do you want me to play it again mummy?" Archie enquired and Meghan's heartwarming response was: "Yes please, thank you."

During Archie's musical rendition, Meghan's hand could be seen rubbing his back in an encouraging motion as he sits on the couple's white couch in their lounge, alongside an Aztec print black cushion.

The tell-all documentary gave the Sussexes a chance to tell their side of the story, as well as give fans an insight into their new life in America, where they reside in Montecito.

Another adorable moment that was chosen for the show was a sweet daddy-son clip where Prince Harry sped down the vast driveway of their private residence with Archie strapped in a seat on the back of the royal's bicycle.

As well as raising two children, Harry and Meghan have three pet dogs at their US family home. Speaking in a virtual appearance, Harry gave an insight into his very busy household, saying: "I tell you what, we all need a dog that keeps us calm. I've got three in this house now. So, we basically have five children." 

Prince Harry is currently away from his US home as he is in the UK  to attend a court case against the publishers of the Daily Mail, ANL (Associated Newspapers Limited).
