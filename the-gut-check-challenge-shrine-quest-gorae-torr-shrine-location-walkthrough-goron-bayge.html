Zelda: Breath of the Wild guide: The Gut Check Challenge shrine quest (Gorae Torr shrine) location a

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 16, 2024

How to find The Gut Check Challenge shrine quest

Grid View

The Gut Check Challenge shrine quest is located in northeastern Hyrule, north of Death Mountain and Akkala Ancient Tech Lab. (Browse the gallery above for the precise location.)

Atop of the appropriately named Gut Check Rock (which is surrounded by healing springs … and also enemies), you’ll find a Goron named Bayge. Speak to him, and he’ll give you The Gut Check Challenge shrine quest.

It involves climbing up the eponymous rock in the allotted time, while collecting 100 rupees. There’s not much to it, frankly. Just plot a course that heads to the rupees on the rock. You may want to equip climbing gear to make your trip faster and have some stamina-replenishing elixirs, just in case.

Do that, speak to Bayge again, and Gorae Torr shrine will appear.

Gorae Torr shrine treasure

There’s nothing to inside of Gorae Torr shrine other than open a treasure chest for a Great Frostblade Durability Up +, and meet Gorae Torr for your spirit orb.
