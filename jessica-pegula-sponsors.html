Jessica Pegula is a professional tennis player, who is currently ranked No.5 in the world by the WTA. She is also ranked 3rd in the world in doubles, ahead of the 2024 season. Pegula has reached the quarter-final of a Grand Slam six times. The American has made one Grand Slam final, in women's doubles. Although she has been a consistent performer on the circuit, 'JP' has not made it past the last eight stage yet.

Jessica Pegula is the daughter of billionaire Terry Pegula, who owns many sports brands like Buffalo Bills, Buffalo Sabres and Rochester Americans. She has started a restaurant named Healthy Scratch with her sister Kelly Pegula. Jessica also has a charity named “A Lending Paw,” and a beauty care brand called “Ready 24.” Pegula married her long-time boyfriend, Taylor Gahagen, in 2021. He is an executive in her family's sports company, Pegula Sports and Entertainment.

Brands like Adidas, Yonex, Ready Nutrition, and Stella Artois sponsor Jessica Pegula. The detailed description of Jessica Pegula's sponsored brands is as follows:

Adidas is one of the largest sports brands that create high-quality athletic footwear, apparel, and other sports accessories. Jessica Pegula wears the Adidas Barricade tennis shoe range for her footwear. In the 2023 French Open, Pegula wore the Adidas Paris match skirt and Tennis Y Tank.

Yonex is a Japanese sports equipment manufacturer famous for tennis racquets, shoes, and other accessories. Pegula signed a racquet sponsorship deal with Yonex, and currently uses the Yonex Ezone 98 racquet.

Ready Nutrition is a sports nutrition company that offers products like protein water, protein bars and sports drinks designed to improve athletes' performance. Jessica Pegula signed a deal with Ready Nutrition in 2022. Famous NBA star Giannis Antetokounmpo also promotes the brand.

Stella Artois is a famous beer brand, and they signed a deal with Jessica Pegula in 2023.
