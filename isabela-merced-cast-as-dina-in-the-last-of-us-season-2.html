Isabela Merced Cast As Dina In 'The Last Of Us' Season 2

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 30, 2024

Isabela Merced is joining the cast of The Last of Us for season 2. The Transformers: The Last Knight star has joined the acclaimed HBO drama’s cast as Dina, a character known to fans from The Last of Us: Part II game. Showrunners Craig Mazin and Neil Druckmann said, “Dina is warm, brilliant, wild, funny, moral, dangerous and instantly lovable. You can search forever for an actor who effortlessly embodies all of those things, or you can find Isabela Merced right away. We couldn’t be prouder to have her join our family.”
