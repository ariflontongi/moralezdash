Lil Pump Shows Off New Face Tattoo

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, August 26, 2024

Lil Pump is someone who has remained relevant over the years, despite all odds. Overall, it is hard to maintain relevance in the social media age. Fans will build you up, and then immediately tear you down. However, Pump has largely been able to survive that. Sure, his music may not get the same sales as "Gucci Gang," but he is still a social media personality that people like. He has displayed growth over the years, and there is no doubt that he has become much more mature as a person.

That said, one thing that has not changed when it comes to Lil Pump is his love of tattoos. One look at his face and you realize that he has spent a lot of money on ink over the years. However, Pump is certainly not alone on this one. There are plenty of other artists who have done the same thing. It is just that Pump in particular is seen as the face, no pun intended, of getting face tattoos. He has quite a few interesting ones, and subsequently, he has added yet another to his collection.

Lil Pump Adds To His Collection

In the Instagram post up above, you can see the latest tattoo to grace Lil Pump's forehead. If you are confused, simply pick out the tat that looks the freshest. That would be the one at the top that seems like a glossy black. It contains what appears to be a tribal crest as well as a star right underneath. Although we aren't too sure what it means, we're positive it means something to Pump. Tattoos are generally an incredibly personal thing. That said, all Lil Pump had to say about it was "Got a new face tat what do u think?"

Whether or not Lil Pump will be getting more face tattoos remains to be seen. After all, he is running out of room on his face.
