How much does it cost to rent a Rug Doctor at Dollar General?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Answer

The short answer is that Rug Doctor carpet cleaners are available for rent at most Dollar General locations. Rental prices for a 24-hour period are around $35, while fees for a 48-hour period are approximately $70. To use the machine, you’ll need to rent brush attachments separately and buy a cleaning solution, both of which are extra costs.

Also inquired about was how much it would cost to hire a Rug Doctor.

Rug Doctor allows you to hire a machine from a partner site (such as Home Depot or Walmart) for roughly $30 a day, depending on the equipment. You’ll need three bottles of the cleaning solution to clean 1,600 square feet of carpet, which will cost you roughly $17 for 48 ounces.
