What’s more, does Maggiano’s family style dining include unlimited food and drink?

With the family style menu, your party will choose two appetisers, two salads, two groups of two entrees (for a total of four entrees), and two desserts from a variety of options. Maggiano’s provides three different family-style meals. There are three appetizers/salads to choose from, as well as two pasta dishes and lemon cookies in the “light” option. For adults, the ticket is $20.95, while children aged 5 to 12 pay $11.95 per person.
