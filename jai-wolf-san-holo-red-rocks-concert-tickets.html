Jai Wolf and San Holo Red Rocks concert: How do I get tickets?

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 25, 2024

JAI Wolf and San Holo are gearing up for the Infinite Light concert and with presale tickets on sale now and general admission tickets just days away fans want to know it all.

The concert is scheduled to be held on June 8, 2022, and feature special guests Manila Killa and TSU Nami.

How do I get tickets for the Jai Wolf and San Holo Red Rocks concert?

Presale tickets are on sale now and can be purchased at AXS.com or by phone at 888-929-7849.

General ticket sales are scheduled to begin on January 28, 2022.

Ticket prices range from $50 to $75 depending on the price.

Where is the concert?

The concert is scheduled to be held at Red Rocks Park and Amphitheatre ten miles west of Denver, Colorado.

The Red Rocks Amphitheatre is known as an open-air amphitheater that is built into a rock structure.

Over the years, a number of musicians have performed there including U2, Widespread Panic, James Taylor, 311, The Eagles, and Coldplay.

more from concerts

Many artists have also released audio and video recordings of their performances due to the popularity of the location.

The Beatles, The Grateful Dead, and Jimi Hendrix have also famously played there.

Who are Jai Wolf and San Holo?

Jai Wolf, whose real name is Sajeeb Saha, 30, is famously known as a New York-based electronic music producer.

Over the years, he has released a number of songs including Indian Summer, Like It's Over, and Starlight.

Wolf is set to embark on a 2022 tour in February and will also have shows in San Francisco, Phoenix, and Okeechobee.

San Holo, whose real name is Sander van Dijck, 31, is known as a Dutch DJ, musician, and record producer from Zoetermeer.

Holo gained international recognition for his remix of Dr. Dre's The Next Episode, which currently has over 250 million views on YouTube.

He is set to embark on the bb u ok? tour in October 2022 in honor of his recent album of the same name and play over 30 shows across the United States.
