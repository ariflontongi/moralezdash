The Best NYFW After-Party Looks

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 5, 2024

You may be laboring under the misguided belief that New York Fashion Week is only about runway shows. That's partially true, but Fashion Week is also about parties. Many, many fancy parties. And while the runways provide one sort of inspiration, the nighttime scene is giving us another. From a big Cartier bash to private parties at 1 Oak to a book launch that attracted a swarm of Victoria's Secret models, here are the best looks from this season's NYFW after-parties.
