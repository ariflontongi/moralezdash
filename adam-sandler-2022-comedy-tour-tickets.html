Adam Sandler fall 2022 comedy tour: Where can I buy tickets?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 26, 2024

ACTOR and comedian Adam Sandler announced he will be going on a national tour only days after he celebrated his 56th birthday.

Sandler is known for his comedic films including The Waterboy, The Wedding Singer, and Grown Ups.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

When is Adam Sandler performing?

Adam Sandler's tour will kick off a 15-city tour beginning in the fall 2022.

His tour will feature an unannounced surprise guest and will start in Allentown, Pennsylvania on October 21.

Several of the stops on his tour will take place in cities where he canceled shows in 2020 amidst the Covid-19 pandemic.

This will be Sandler's first tour since 2019 following the release of his first Netflix comedy special, 100% Fresh in 2018.

The tour will wrap up in Savannah, Georgia on November 14, 2022.

Where is Adam Sandler performing?

Sandler's tour will take place across 15 cities including:

  • October 21 at PPL Center (Allentown, Pennsylvania)
  • October 22 at SNHU Arena (Manchester, New Hampshire)
  • October 23 at MGM Music Hall at Fenway (Boston, Massachusetts)
  • October 25 at Turning Stone Resort Casino Event Center (Verona, New York)
  • October 27 at UBS Arena (Belmont Park, New York)
  • October 28 at Hard Rock Live at Etess Arena (Atlantic City, New Jersey)
  • October 29 at Mohegan Sun Arena (Uncasville, Connecticut)
  • November 6 at Amalie Arena (Tampa, Florida)
  • November 7 at Hertz Arena (Estero, Florida)
  • November 9 at Hard Rock Live (Hollywood, Florida)
  • November 10 at St. Augustine Amphitheatre (St. Augustine, Florida)
  • November 11 at Bon Secours Wellness Arena (Greenville, South Carolina)
  • November 12 at Gas South Arena (Duluth, Georgia)
  • November 13 at PNC Arena (Raleigh, North Carolina)
  • November 14 at Enmarket Arena Savannah (Savannah, Georgia)

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for Adam Sandler's tour will officially go on sale on September 16 at 10am local time through Ticketmaster.

They will also be available for pre-sale on September 15 at 10am local time exclusively on Ticketmaster. Those wishing to purchase pre-sale tickets can do so using the code: VENUE.

Tickets will also be available for purchase on SeatGeek and VividSeats.
