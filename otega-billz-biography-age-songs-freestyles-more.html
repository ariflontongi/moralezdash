Otega Billz Biography

Stephen Ifedayo Oghenetega famously known as Otega Billz, once a blogger is a 29 years old Nigerian rapper, singer, and songwriter who gained a massive breakthrough in his single “Stay Woke” which featured CDQ.

Another song that confirmed the street rapper is a great singer and not in the industry for a joke was “Aje” which he got featured on; the song originally owned by Diamond Jimma which highlighted Otega Billz saw the talented artist showcasing his impeccable lyrics.

Otega Billz is signed under the record label “Advert Gang”

Background & Age

The creative street musical artist Otega Billz was born on October 16, 1994, in Ijebu Ode; an area in Ogun State Nigeria but a native of Edo State. He grew up in the same city he was born.

Musical Career

Otega Billz was once a known blogger who happened to use the earnings he got from the blogging business to support his music career. Obtaining OND in Computer science in Abraham Adesanya Polytechnic, Ijebu Igbo, Ogun State.

Otega Billz not giving up was one of the keynotes that skyrocketed the artist’s music career to where he is today. Otega started singing in 2010, getting massive fans from the street to date.

Otega Billz as he fondly called himself after several freestyles and tracks recorded, featured CDQ in his well-accepted single “Stay Woke (Ji Masun).“

That didn’t just got him famous but moved his music career up and served as a sort of motivation for him to hold on to what he believed in. After the major breakthrough, the artist got featured in several singles and he did well in those tracks which called good music lovers’ attention to rate him as one of the top talents to watch out for.
