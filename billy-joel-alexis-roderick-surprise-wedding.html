Billy Joel marries Alexis Roderick in surprise wedding

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 16, 2024

    Billy Joel and his girlfriend Alexis Roderick hosted a fourth of July party to remember on Saturday. The couple, who are expecting their first child together later this summer, tied the knot in a surprise ceremony on Saturday evening.

    The singer's spokeswoman confirmed that the couple had got married with a statement to magazine, saying Billy and Alexis "surprised guests at their annual July 4th party by exchanging vows in front of their family and close friends."

    Billy Joel and Alexis Roderick tied the knot on Saturday

    The intimate ceremony was held at Billy's estate on Long Island, with his daughter Alexa Ray and actor Kevin James among the small group of guests. The nuptials were led by New York Governor Andrew Cuomo, a long-time friend of the singer.

    A photo released from the couple's big day sees them looking blissfully happy together. Billy can be seen gazing into the camera wearing a dark blue suit and tie, while Alexis has her eyes closed and cosies up to her new husband. The bride wore a traditional strapless wedding gown and clutched a floral bouquet while showing off her new wedding ring.

    Billy and Alexis' wedding comes less than three months after they announced they are expecting their first child together. It has since been revealed that it will be another daughter for Billy.

    The couple are expecting their first child together this summer

    Speaking to , the source added: "They want to call the baby Madison."

    The singer met Alexis, a former adviser to investment firm Morgan Stanley at a restaurant. In 2010 Alexis told the that they "were friends for a while before we started dating".

    Billy, a six-time Grammy winner, was previously married to Katie Lee in the early 2000s. His first marriage to Elizabeth Weber ended in the 1980s, before he met and married supermodel Christie Brinkley. The couple split in 1994.
