How to Stream The Curse Online and Watch Without Cable for Free

Posted by Tandra Barner on Friday, August 9, 2024

Emma Stone and Nathan Fielder play a married couple who aspire to HGTV stardom while trying to conceive a child and facing a supposed hex in The Curse, a limited dark comedy series co-created by Fielder and Benny Safdie. Called an “exhilarating, exhausting cringe comedy” by The Hollywood Reporter‘s Daniel Feinberg, the A24 and Showtime co-produced show’s first episode is available to stream now on Paramount+ with Showtime, while the series premieres on cable on Showtime this Sunday.

Paramount+ with Showtime is also available as an add-on at Amazon’s Prime Video, which offers a free seven-day trial of the network; after that, it auto-renews at $11.99 per month. Those with subscriptions to streaming and internet-based cable TV services, such as DirecTV Stream or Hulu, can add Showtime to their package for an extra $10.99 per month in addition to base plan prices.

Watch The Curse Without Cable: Premiere Date and Time, Episode Release Schedule

The 10-episode dark comedy series The Curse begins streaming today on Paramount+ with Showtime and premieres on cable this Sunday at 10 p.m. ET/PT. Subsequent episodes will follow the same cadence with each part available to stream first every Friday on Paramount+ with Showtime before airing on Showtime on Sundays at 10 p.m. ET/PT. The limited series’ finale streams on Jan. 12, 2024, and broadcasts on Jan. 14, 2024.

For those who already have an existing live TV streaming service subscription, Paramount+ is available as an add-on channel for $10.99-$11.99 per month (on top of the base plan price) through Prime Video, DirecTV Stream and Hulu, to name a few. See more options for watching The Curse and other Showtime content without cable below.

Where to Watch The Curse With Cable

As mentioned above, The Curse airs on the premium cable network Showtime starting this Sunday at 10 p.m. ET/PT. Subsequent episodes will be broadcast weekly on the same day and time, with the finale airing Jan. 14, 2024.

You can watch the dark satire limited series through your cable TV provider, or on ShowtimeAnytime.com with your cable TV account login information.

How to Watch The Curse Outside of the U.S.

Paramount+ is available in select countries outside of the U.S. — so if you’re outside of the country, you can use a virtual private network (VPN) such as ExpressVPN to stream the series premiere of The Curse with your subscription. The service lets you legally access U.S. web servers — even if you’re in another country — to stream on a smart TV, smartphone, tablet or laptop. Learn more about ExpressVPN here.

The Curse: Cast, Series Synopsis

The Curse follows Whitney and Asher Siegel (played by Stone and Fielder), “a newlywed couple struggling to make their vision for eco-living a reality in a small town in New Mexico,” per the show’s logline. Safdie plays Dougie, a producer of a HGTV-like reality show who wants to make their dream home come true. On top of that, the couple is also trying to conceive a child while an alleged curse threatens their relationship.

The comedy series also stars Corbin Bernsen, Constance Shulman, Hikmah Warsame, Dahabo Ahmed, Christopher Calderon, Barkhad Abdi, Gary Farmer and others.

Stream The Curse online starting today at Paramount+ with Showtime, or tune into Showtime on Sunday for the series’ on-air premiere at 10 p.m. ET/PT.
