Selena Gomez Debuted New Bling Seemingly Dedicated to Boyfriend Benny Blanco

Posted by Patria Henriques on Sunday, August 25, 2024

In case it wasn't abundantly clear before, it is now: Selena Gomez is dating music producer Benny Blanco. And the proof is in her new bling. 

Hours after basically confirming that the two were, in fact, a couple on Thursday, the singer took to her Instagram Story to share a loved-up photo of herself with a man that appeared to be Blanco. Selena then followed up her post with a black-and-white snap of her hand adorned with a diamond ring that was seemingly dedicated to her new boyfriend. In the image, Selena wore an open wrapped ring with one prong featuring a collection of pave diamonds in the shape of a B, and the other, a solid princess-cut stone. 

Gomez's latest piece of jewelry was complemented by a dark manicure, and was captured during what appeared to be a dinner date between herself and Blanco, as there were two restaurant menus on a white tablecloth in the background. 

According to a source at Entertainment Tonight, Selena and Benny have been "casually seeing each other for awhile," but only recently "became more official." 

"Things have been going really well between them and Selena is happy," the insider told the outlet, adding, "She thinks Benny is very funny and of course, extremely talented. Selena is in a great place in her life and feels content and relaxed. She is enjoying where things are going.‚Äù
