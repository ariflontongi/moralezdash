Lil Baby &amp; 42 Dugg Take Over The Street In &quot;We Paid&quot;

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Every time that Lil Baby and 42 Dugg link up on a record, it's a competition. Who will go harder? We know that both of them are going to bring it but their collaborations have been a high point of the last year in rap, proving to many why the Detroit up-and-comer deserves to be mentioned in the same breath as Lil Baby. 

Signed to Lil Baby's 4PF label, plenty of opportunities will arise for 42 Dugg to work with a man that he considers his brother. Their most recent collaboration came on the deluxe edition of My Turn, which released last week. 

Serving as the sole feature on the deluxe, 42 Dugg starts off "We Paid" before Lil Baby takes over. They seamlessly bounce from one to the next and now, the track has earned a new video.

Likely filmed before the quarantine began, Lil Baby and 42 Dugg are surrounded by their homies in the new music video. They take over the entire street, locking things down and having some fun in their luxury whips.
