How do you maximize rune gain in Elden Ring?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 9, 2024

In order to maximize your rune gain in Elden Ring, there are several strategies you can employ:

  • Find the Golden Scarab! This talisman improves your rune gain by 20% when equipped.
  • Shatter the glowing white skulls you see around the landscape. These enemies drop five times their usual amount of runes.
  • Keep an eye out for Gold Leaves! These rare consumables can be found throughout the game and boost your rune income for a short time when used.
  • Use the Gold-Pickled Fowl Foot, a consumable that boosts your rune acquisition by 30% for 180 seconds.
  • Farm runes at Mohgwyn’s Palace. This late-game area offers a high level quest that provides a great opportunity for rune farming.
  • Defeat enemies repeatedly to farm runes. Enemies are the main source of runes, and defeating them will reward you with both runes and items.
  • Let enemies battle each other instead of directly engaging them. This can provide you with an opportunity to acquire more runes.
  • Equip the Sacred Relic Sword to optimize your rune farming. This weapon has a special move with a wide area of effect that can expedite the process.
  • Farm at Stormhill, a location accessible early in the game where you can defeat enemies for runes.
  • Defeat the sleeping dragon found in Caelid. This dragon drops Dragon Hearts and 75,000 runes when killed.
  • Equip the Gold Scarab talisman to increase your rune acquisition by approximately 20%.
  • Use Lord’s Runes to obtain 50,000 runes when used or sold.
  • Utilize guards wearing blackish armor to make money quickly in Elden Ring.
