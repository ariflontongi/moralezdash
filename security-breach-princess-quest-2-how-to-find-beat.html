Princess Quest 2: How To Find and Beat in FNAF Security Breach

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Here's how to beat Princess Quest II in Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach.

Page Updated September 6, 2023, 16:27 | First Published August 29, 2023, 16:57

Completing the Princess Quest arcade games in Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach allows you to access certain endings of the game, making them definitely worth a play. They also delve into the lore of FNAF, giving players more information and insights on the series, its story, and its characters. Now, let's take a deeper look at Princess Quest II in FNAF: Security Breach, including where to find it and how to beat it.

Table of Contents

Where Is Princess Quest 2?

To find Princess Quest II, you'll need to go to the Fazcade. Here, down the hallway, you'll find a security office; next to it, you'll find another hallway that will lead you to the second Princess Quest arcade cabinet in the game.

How to Beat Princess Quest 2

Now that you're playing Princess Quest II, you'll need to explore an environment while fighting back enemies using a sword. After entering the first door, take down all of the enemies, grab the chest, and head through the next door into another area. The next room has some enemies and then a maze - go down, left, up, down to get to your next destination.

Now we're back with more fire pits like we saw in the first area, but we won't light these particular ones yet. There's another doorway down the path in this room; THIS is where you'll light some fire pits, giving you a chance to get into the chest and grab a key.

Now, using the fire pit bridge, go back to the outdoor area you were in before and unlock the door using your new key.

Even more fire pits - yay. Light the red one first, then purple using its shadow, and finally, light the orange one.

Now you're in a maze, with fire pits all around. Find them (there are some on all sides of the maze), and unlock the door at the end. Enter this room, then use the fires to create a circle, using the light's shadows to line them up. This can take a little trial and error, but you should be able to figure it out in time.

Once you've completed this relatively challenging puzzle and maze, you can finally exit the game through the security office where you entered. Now that you've completed Princess Quest I and II, you can move on to the end of the game and complete the final Princess Quest III if you're aiming for a certain FNAF: Security Breach ending.
