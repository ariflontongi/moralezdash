Is Matthew Perry Having Kids?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Matthew Perry is a famous Canadian-American actor, comedian, and writer best known for his portrayal of Chandler Bing on the hit television sitcom “Friends.”

Born on August 19, 1969, in Williamstown, Massachusetts, Perry grew up in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada.

His father, John Bennett Perry, was a Canadian actor, while his mother, Suzanne Marie Morrison, was a former Canadian press secretary.

Perry’s acting career began in earnest in the late 1980s when he moved to Los Angeles to pursue his dreams.

He landed several small roles in television shows such as “Charles in Charge” and “Growing Pains” before getting his big break as Chandler Bing in “Friends” in 1994.

“Friends” quickly became one of the most successful television shows of all time, and Perry’s character, Chandler Bing, quickly became a fan favorite.

He earned a Primetime Emmy nomination in 2002 for his work on the show.

Perry has also appeared in several movies throughout his career, including “The Whole Nine Yards,” “Fools Rush In,” and “17 Again.”

In addition to acting, he has also worked as a writer and producer, creating and executive producing the short-lived sitcom “Mr. Sunshine” in 2011.

Despite his success, Perry has struggled with addiction throughout his life and has been open about his struggles with substance abuse.

He has been in and out of rehab several times and has used his experiences to raise awareness and advocate for addiction recovery.
