Kim Kardashian Is Sending Taylor Swift a Valentine's Day Gift

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 16, 2024

To celebrate the launch of her new KKW Beauty Kimoji Hearts fragrances, Kim Kardashian is sending out gift boxes of the new scent to family members, friends, beauty editors, and other VIPs. But she's not only reaching out to her besties, she's surprising "haters," too—yes, including Taylor Swift.

"I decided for this Valentine's Day, everyone deserves a valentine, and I'm going to send them to my lovers, to my haters, to everyone that I think of, because it's Valentine's Day after all," Kardashian said on her Instagram story today, while revealing a list of people she's sending a gift box to.

Kim Kardashian / Instagram Story

It won't take long for fans to notice that the blue list all the way to the left consists of people who've publicly thrown shade or feuded Kardashian in the past. Swift's name sticks out on the list, after she fought with Kim and Kanye throughout 2016 over the rapper's controversial "Famous" lyrics and that video of Swift approving them over a phone call.

Blac Chyna, Rob Kardashian's ex who's had legal drama with the Kardashian family, and critics of Kim's infamous 2016 naked selfie—Pink, Chloe Grace Moretz, Peirs Morgan, and Bette Middler—also made the list.

Other names on the list are: Sarah Michelle Gellar and Janice Dickinson (who publicly criticized one of Kim and Kanye's magazine covers), Chelsea Handler (who's often poked fun at the Kardashians), Naya Rivera (who dissed Kim's famous Paper magazine 2014 cover), Wendy Williams (who slammed the Kim's recent nude Instagrams), and Sharon Osbourne (who called Kim a "ho"—ouch).

This is what the Kimoji recipients can expect to receive in the mail, courtesy of Paris Hilton:

These are the friends and family members in Kim's "lovers" list: Cher, Jennifer Lawrence, Chrissy Teigen, Anna Wintour, Lala (Anthony), Desi Perkins, Lindsay Lohan, Kylie Jenner, Paris Hilton, Ciara, Cardi B, Khloé Kardashian, Teyana Taylor, Jen Atkin, Mariah Carey, Chris Jenner, Mario Dedivanovic, Amrezy (Amra Olević), Hailey Baldwin, Kourtney Kardashian, Coco Austin, Kendall Jenner, Karrueche, and Kim Zolciak.

Kardashian noted that the people she listed in her Instagram story aren't the only ones getting a present, so plenty more of Kim's friends (and frenemies) can expect a surprise delivery.
