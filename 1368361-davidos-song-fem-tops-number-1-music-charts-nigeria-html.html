Davidos song FEM tops at number 1 on all music charts in Nigeria

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 24, 2024

- Top Nigerian singer, Davido’s long break from social media to focus on his music seems to have paid off

- The music star’s new track, FEM, has already garnered great success in just a few weeks after its release

- According to Turntable Charts, the song was number one on all weekly charts in Nigeria

- Youtube, Shazam, Apple Music, Boomplay, Triller, iTunes and more were the platforms where the track did well

The year 2020 seems to be going quite well for top Nigerian singer, Davido, and his music career. His newly released track, FEM, has been accepted quite well by his fans.

According to Turntable Charts, Davido has broken a record by being the first artiste to lead on all platforms in a week.

Turntable said that it had been tracking the singer’s newly released track, FEM, and that it debuted at number one on all weekly charts and on all platforms.
