Fox Pulls John Schneider From 'Masked Singer' Press Interviews After Joe Biden Tweet

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 30, 2024

The Hollywood Reporter has learned Fox canceled press appearances for John Schneider the morning after exit from The Masked Singer over a social media post about President Joe Biden. Fox had no comment in the wake of the Dukes of Hazzard star calling on social media for the public executions of President Biden and his son Hunter Biden.
