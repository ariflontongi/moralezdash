Does Intermix hold seasonal sales?

Absolutely, there are regular sales throughout the year including Black Friday and Cyber Monday. Consider signing up for emails and text alerts to be the first to know about promotions. How long is the returns policy at Intermix?

If for any reason you aren’t totally satisfied with your order you have up to 30 days from the date of receipt. The great news is that returns are complimentary, Intermix cover the cost! A full refund will be made to the original payment method used.

Is tracking possible at Intermix?

Of course! If you’re a member, even better, you can simply log in to your account to access tracking information.
