Do you sweep before you Swiffer?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Don't Swiffer sweep without vacuuming first

The Swiffer Sweeper pads will do a better job picking up dirt on floors when the larger dust bunnies are out of the way, so a quick pick-up or vacuum job beforehand could help your duster pack a mightier punch.

Do I need to sweep before Swiffer?

Do I need to vacuum before using the Swiffer Sweeper wet pads or Swiffer WetJet? Yes, it's wise to either vacuum or sweep first. Otherwise, the wet pads can push large debris around rather than clean it up.

Does Swiffer replace sweeping?

Using the Swiffer Sweeper is straightforward and simple. The sweeper is meant to replace a broom, dustpan and mop.
