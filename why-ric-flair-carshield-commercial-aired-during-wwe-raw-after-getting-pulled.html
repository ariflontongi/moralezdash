Why Ric Flair CarShield Commercial Aired During WWE RAW After Getting Pulled

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 16, 2024

Ric Flair generated ton of controversy when the Plane Ride From Hell was exposed for all to see. His antics of exposing himself and allegedly making a flight attendant touch him was also let out. This caused for many to call on cancellation of The Nature Boy. CarShield took action and paused their commercial campaign with Flair, but that ad still ran during RAW this week.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE RAW this week.

Despite Ric Flair’s CarShield commercial airing during RAW, it was still pulled. They simply didn’t have the ability to take it out of the rotation that swiftly. Dave Meltzer noted on Wrestling Observer Radio that it takes a bit of time for advertisements to really go away due to how they are set up.

“They still had the commercial tonight on RAW. They pulled it, but you can’t pull it immediately. You know it takes a couple of days. I saw it over the weekened a couple of times, and I saw it during RAW.”

Odds are fans will see less and less of that CarShield campaign as time goes by. Very soon fans might never see them again. The company did not specify how long they are pausing the campaign, but this heat on the two-time WWE Hall of Famer doesn’t seem to be letting up.
